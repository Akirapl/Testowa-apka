Instructions:
1. Open ZENIT in your browser
2. Click the "MUZEUM" button
3. Copy the JSON above (entire string between ---START--- and ---END---)
4. Paste it into the "KOD QR KURATORA" text field
5. Click "Wczytaj wystawę"

You should now see the first artwork (Nocny spacer) and be able to navigate through the exhibition.

---

For creating your own QR codes:
- Prepare a JSON following the format above
- Generate a QR code from the JSON (use any QR generator online)
- Print/display the QR code at your museum exhibition
- Visitors scan it and ZENIT loads your exhibition data

Enjoy!
